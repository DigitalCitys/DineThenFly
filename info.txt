DineThenFly by Giulia Spier

A mobile app that shows users a list of an airport's dining locations, their locations, and their menus.

The purpose of DineThenFly is to make it easier for airport goers to purchase food before their flights, especially in larger airports.

Version 1.0.0 - Basic Information

Shows a list of dining locations, their locations in the airport, their opening/closing times (depending on the day of the week) and their menus. If the restaurant is a chain (McDonalds, Starbucks, etc.), the user shall be redirected to the chain's website.
